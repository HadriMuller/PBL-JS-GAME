🎮 How to play

  1. 👨‍🍳 Customer Orders: Check the customer's order — don’t mess up or they'll get mad!
  2. 🍞 Prepare the Food: Click the correct ingredients to the prep area.
  3. 🗑️ Trash Mistakes: Drop any wrong ingredients in the trash bin. Clean cooking = clean score.
  4. ✅ Serve Fast: Tap the Serve button to deliver food before your customer loses it.
  5. ⏱️ Beat the Timer: Every second counts — if the clock hits zero, your customer gets freaky 😵.
  6. 🧠 Score System: Earn 15 points per perfect order. If 5 customers get mad, it’s game over!
